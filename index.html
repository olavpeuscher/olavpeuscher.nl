Projects

A selection of personal work over the years.

DIY 2-way Seas speakers

DIY floor-standing loudspeakers based on SEAS drivers, Mundorf and ClaityCap caps and Jantzen coils. Transparent but warm sound in a stylish designed case with oak wooden front.

Oil painting

Painting made with oil-paint, decorated by a simple but stylish self made oak wooden frame. There is not a real story behind the painting, except the orange colour being inspired by sunset since I love the view of sunset. The colours were blended making use of a trowel, adding some warmth to my living room.

Minimalistic analoque amplifiers

DIY pre-amplifier with mathing mono amplifier. The pre-amplifier was designed to switch channels and control the front-, center- and subwoofer speakers seperately with one master volume.
